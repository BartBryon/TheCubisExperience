A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Hey everyone,

my name is Bart . I'm a last year Information Management & Security student at the Thomas More College in Mechelen. I've been specializing in Cyber Security for the past 2,5 years. It's a fascinating subject and indispensible in today's business world.

During my studies I came in contact with the other specialization in our course, Data Management. Data has become a more valuable asset than oil and this intrigued me for two reasons:

-   It meant that this seamingly useless data can be gathered and analyzed and turned into a marketable asset.
-   It's one of the world's largest markets and I just wanted to know what it was all about.

I started messing around with it. Trying out software like Tableau and Power Bi. It turned out that I really enjoyed the process of working with these tools and data in general. That's Why I chose to follow an internship at Cubis Solutions, A Business Intelligence Company that consults clients on what to do whith their huge amounts of gathered data.

The topic of my internship is Data Virtualization. For the companies that have tonnes of data but lack the time/ability to extract, transform or load their data, data virtualization might be the ideal option. So for the next 3 months I wil be comparing SAP's HANA and Power BI's ability for Direct Queries.

I'm looking forward to it and I'm ready to learn!
